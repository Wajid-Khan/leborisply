About Us

India’s Trusted Name in Plywood & Natural Veneers

Leboris Ply is the only veneer company in India dedicated exclusively to manufacturing 100% natural veneers. As pioneers in plywood and veneer manufacturing, we have redefined industry standards through continuous innovation and uncompromised quality.

With decades of expertise, Leboris has revolutionised modern living by enhancing space concepts and delivering contemporary lifestyle solutions. Our extensive product portfolio evolves constantly to match changing consumer preferences.

Trusted by leading architects and designers, Leboris plywood and veneers stand the test of time across residential, commercial and industrial applications.
