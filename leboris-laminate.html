Leboris Laminates

Experience surfaces felt never before! Leboris Laminates is a leader in the Indian premium laminate segment, manufacturing export-quality laminates with a versatile range of designer, digital, foil, leather, and classic collections.

Our latest finishes include Zero Finish, Lustrous, Natural Stone, Sparkle, and Cement Touch, giving a charismatic appeal to laminate grains while delivering real value for money.

Leboris is the only laminate company offering laminates in 9×4 and 10×4 sizes, enabling jointless shutters up to 10 feet height.
