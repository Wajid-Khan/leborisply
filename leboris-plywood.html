About Leboris Ply

Premium affordable plywood has always been a driving force at Leboris Plywood Industries. We manufacture a wide range of premium quality plywood at modest pricing.

Our team constantly studies new market trends to fulfil needs of channel partners and end users. We invest heavily in infrastructure and R&D to support innovative manufacturing.

Leboris plywood and blockboards are known for durability, strength and eco-friendly quality.

Guidelines for Stress Free Furniture

  • Ensure plywood is not placed in water or moisture at site.
  • Laminate outer surface of cabinet to avoid termite entry.
  • Leave half inch gap between wall and cabinet.
  • Do termite treatment before flooring work.
  • Maintain cabinet cleanliness by wiping with dry cloth.
  • Buy plywood from verified Leboris dealer.
  • Choose from 450+ natural veneer species.
  • Always prefer quality plywood.
